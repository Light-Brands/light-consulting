## Risk Management

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Migration complexity | Medium | High | Phased approach, extensive testing |
| AI quality issues | Medium | High | Human review gates, quality metrics |
| Platform stability | Low | High | Redundancy, monitoring, rollback |

### Business Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Client resistance | Medium | Medium | Clear communication, gradual rollout |
| Team transition | Medium | Medium | Training, new role definitions |
| Market timing | Low | Medium | Flexibility in roadmap |

### Mitigation Strategy

1. **Parallel Operation**: Keep WordPress running during transition
2. **Pilot First**: Prove value with select clients before broad rollout
3. **Quality Gates**: Human review at critical checkpoints
4. **Rollback Plans**: Clear procedures to revert if needed
5. **Client Communication**: Transparent updates throughout
