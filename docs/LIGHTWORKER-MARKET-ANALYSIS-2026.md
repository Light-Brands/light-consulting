## Part 1: Market Definition & Segmentation

### Who Are Light Workers?

Light workers are individuals who feel called to a purpose greater than themselves—to heal, teach, guide, create, or transform. They are:

| Segment | Description | Size Estimate |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| **Spiritual Coaches & Healers** | Energy healers, intuitive coaches, meditation teachers, shamanic practitioners | 2.5M globally |
| **Wellness Practitioners** | Life coaches, health coaches, holistic therapists, yoga instructors | 5.8M globally |
| **Conscious Entrepreneurs** | Purpose-driven founders building impact-first businesses | 15M globally |
| **Transformation Guides** | Course creators, retreat leaders, authors, speakers in personal growth | 3.2M globally |
| **Emerging Light Workers** | Individuals sensing a calling but not yet launched | 50M+ globally |

### Market Characteristics

**What Unifies This Market:**
- Mission-driven over profit-driven (yet need financial sustainability)
- Values-centered decision making
- Relationship and community orientation
- Often solo or micro-business operators
- High skill in their craft, low skill in business building
- Resistance to traditional "pushy" marketing
- Desire for authentic expression over manufactured positioning

**The Core Tension:**
> "I want to serve the world, but I don't know how to build a business without compromising my values."

This tension is the single biggest opportunity in the market.
