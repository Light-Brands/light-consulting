# B2B Enterprise Strategy: Agencies, Accelerators, VCs & Consulting Firms

**Light Brand Consulting | Strategic Go-To-Market Plan**
**Created: January 2026**
**Core Message: AI or Die**

---

## Executive Summary

Following our successful deal with Influex, this document outlines a comprehensive 12-month strategy to systematically target and convert B2B service organizations—brand agencies, startup accelerators, VC firms, and consulting firms—into Light Brand clients.

These organizations share a critical characteristic: **they advise others on transformation while being vulnerable to disruption themselves.** Our message—"AI or Die"—resonates deeply because these firms see AI reshaping their clients' industries and know they're next.

**The Opportunity**: These firms have:
- High margins that can fund transformation
- Networks that create referral cascades
- Urgent need to differentiate in commoditizing markets
- Internal knowledge that AI can systematize
- Client delivery that AI can scale

**The Strategy**: Position Light Brand as the partner that helps these firms evolve from "using AI tools" to "being AI-native organizations"—exactly what they need to remain relevant and what Influex bought.
